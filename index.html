About PawMediq

PawMediq is a student-based web project designed to demonstrate how an online pet medicine system works. It provides a cart system, order history tracking, and medicine browsing using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Our Services

  • Online pet medicine ordering
  • Supplement and healthcare products
  • Simple shopping cart system
  • Order history management
  • Search-based medicine browsing

Advantages of PawMediq

  • Fast and simple ordering process
  • No registration required
  • Clean and beginner-friendly interface
  • Works offline after loading
  • Stores cart and history in browser
